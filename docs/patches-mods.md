# Patches & Mods
Certain mods require standalone patches to work properly with GTS.

All patches here are hosted on the [Black Book's GitHub](https://github.com/hexanode0x0/gts-black-book).

Patches for paid mods cannot be uploaded to Nexus Mods at all.
